01 Who this is for not everyone, on purpose

ICP

Anchored on personal brands — open to real business brands too, as long as the person behind it thinks the way I do: founder-first, not the other way around.

Who they are

  • Young guys building a personal brand around real skill
  • Coaches / online service sellers, mainly
  • Founders scaling a brand, founder-led like mine

The gap

  • Conversion isn't working
  • No real structure behind their content
  • Don't know how to sell without feeling gross about it
  • Knowledge and skill are there — representation isn't

Mindset filter

  • Willing to invest money, not just time
  • Ready to put in the actual work
  • Wants their full potential unlocked, not a quick patch

Hard no

  • No "guru" hype sellers
  • No one selling false promises to desperate people
  • If it feels like a scam, it's a no — regardless of budget
Scope note — not micro-niching yet. Coaches and founders are the center of gravity, not a hard wall.
